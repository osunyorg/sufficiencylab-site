Sufficiency PhD Days

The concept of sufficiency has deep philosophical roots, dating back to Ancient Greece. Its emergence as a modern academic framework, however, is more recent. Despite growing momentum, recent applications of sufficiency often place disproportionate emphasis on demand reduction , overlooking critical aspects of social justice and basic well-being .
