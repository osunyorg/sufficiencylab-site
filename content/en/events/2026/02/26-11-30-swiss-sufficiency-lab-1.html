As part of the World Sufficiency Lab’s “Sufficiency 1st Consortium,” we’re delighted to announce the launch of the Swiss Sufficiency Lab (SSL), our first National Sufficiency Lab, hosted at Empa's Urban Energy Systems Laboratory.

Register here, and join us for an exciting launch, featuring A keynote on Swiss sufficiency potentials by the World Sufficiency Lab followed by a round table discussion with inspiring voices across academia, governance, and philanthropy, including Prof. Julia Steinberger, Cédric Jeanneret, Dr. Marcel Hänggi and Joséphine von Mitschke-Collande.
