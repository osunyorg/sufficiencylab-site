Switzerland’s Sufficiency Gap Report and Swiss Sufficiency Lab (SSL)


The  Swiss Sufficiency Lab  (SSL) is the first  National Sufficiency Lab  (WSL) established by the World Sufficiency Lab as part of its  Sufficiency 1st Consortium .    This launch marks a significant milestone in the effort to advance sufficiency research, policy innovation, and public understanding at the national level. To support the creation of the Swiss Sufficiency Lab, the World Sufficiency Lab team has produced a dedicated report titled Switzerland's Sufficiency Gap: Geoeconomic
Metamorphosis Ahead . This report provides an in‑depth analysis of Switzerland's sufficiency landscape and highlights the structural transformations required to align the country with sufficiency‑driven pathways.   The SSL is hosted at Empa's  Urban Energy Systems Laboratory .
