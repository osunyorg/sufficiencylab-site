Step into a future where sufficiency feels abundant, inspiring, and actionable, and leave equipped to co-create urban lives that thrive on quality, rather than quantity.

body: >-


Most urban sustainability efforts
focus on efficiency and technological fixes. This event offers a different
perspective by exploring how cities can thrive within planetary boundaries
while prioritizing quality of life over ever-increasing consumption. It brings
together participants from across sectors to explore new pathways toward urban
sustainability.

 

The evening will include:

a guided tour of Empa's NEST innovation building

a multistakeholder dialogue circle

a hands-on future-making workshop

a networking apéro.  The event is open to participants
from all sectors, and no prior knowledge is required. It will be held in
English.

 
The event is hosted by

the Swiss Sufficiency Lab , the Urban Energy Systems Laboratory (UESL) at Empa, and the Sustainability Innovation Lab at the University of St. Gallen.      You can find more information and the  registration link here .
