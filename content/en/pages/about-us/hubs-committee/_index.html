The Hubs Committee is composed of representatives from each regional/national hub. 

The Hubs Committee assists the WSL Founders in identifying the national/regional priorities and opportunities for making sufficiency first in decision making. 

The Hubs Committee has three major tasks:

  • To deliver an opinion on how the WSL multiannual and annual work programme contributes to meeting national/regional priorities; 
  • To enhance collaboration on sufficiency across regions
  • To support the WSL in identifying sufficiency practices, policies and challenges in each region

To deliver on the above, and to discuss topics of mutual interest to support the work of the WSL, the Hubs Committee meets three times per year (online).
