Sufficiency community is a community of communities gathering policymakers, industry leaders, scientists, advocates, and media globally in a dedicated forum and social network. The aim is to encourage regional/national and thematic collaboration, discussion, and coordination around sufficiency projects, letting users create open or permissioned groups, from the local to the global scale.
