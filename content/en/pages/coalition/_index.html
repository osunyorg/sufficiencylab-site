body: >-


The Sufficiency First Coalition aims at mainstreaming the sufficiency concept at the international level by centralizing the research conduced by the National Sufficiency Labs and the different Sufficiency Leaders. The ultimate target is to build a global sufficiency community that will ensure sufficiency is considered first in policy and business decisions. Using community organizing principles, this coalition will create opportunities for online and in-person collaboration and stimulate active involvement and knowledge co-creation of the stakeholders involved in the sufficiency community.
