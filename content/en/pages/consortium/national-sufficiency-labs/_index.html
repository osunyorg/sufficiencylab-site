National Sufficiency Labs aim at mainstreaming the sufficiency concept at the national levels. United by a shared commitment to ecological integrity and social equity, the Consortium brings together interdisciplinary teams from across countries to explore how societies can fleurish within planetary boundaries. By fostering dialogue, sharing methodologies, and co-producing knowledge, the Sufficiency-First Consortium aims to strengthen the scientific foundation for sufficiency-based approaches and support their integration into public discourse and decision-making.
