Sufficiency First Coalition aims at mainstreaming the sufficiency concept at international, regional, and national levels.

The ultimate target is to build a global sufficiency community that will ensure sufficiency is considered first in policy and business decisions. 

Using community organizing principles, Sufficiency First Coalition will create opportunities for online and in-person collaboration and stimulate active involvement and knowledge co-creation of the stakeholders involved in the sufficiency community.
