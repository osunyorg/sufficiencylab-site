The World Sufficiency Lab

The World Sufficiency Lab is a non-profit and independent Paris-based think-to-do tank, whose mission is to serve as the primary resource and voice for sufficiency worldwide in order to reach a world in which sufficiency is first in all policy and business decisions to keep the planet healthy and liveable for all. To accomplish its mission, the World Sufficiency Lab pursues the following objectives:
Filling sufficiency research gaps. 
Building a community of trained sufficiency ambassadors.
Working on the adoption and implementation of sufficiency policies. 

Scientific Committee

The Scientific Committee is composed of members of high scientific standing in sufficiency and aim at providing scientific advice and opinions on any scientific matter. Members of the Scientific Committee are shown on this page.

Sufficiency First Coalitions

The Sufficiency First Coalitions aim at mainstreaming the sufficiency concept at the international level by centralizing the research conduced by the National Sufficiency Labs.  


Coalitions are currently being developed. If you want to stay informed, follow our activities on our LinkedIn page; and if you would like to play an active role, take a look at the document below:)

National Sufficiency Labs

The Sufficiency Labs aim at mainstreaming the sufficiency concept at the national levels.  


National Sufficiency Labs are currently being developed. If you want to stay informed, follow our activities on our LinkedIn page; and if you would like to play an active role, take a look at the document below:)
