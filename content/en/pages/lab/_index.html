The World Sufficiency Lab is a non-profit and independent Paris-based think-to-do tank, whose  mission is to serve as the  primary resource and voice for sufficiency worldwide in order to reach a world in which sufficiency is first in all policy and business decisions to keep the planet healthy and liveable for all .  To accomplish its mission, the World Sufficiency Lab pursues the following objectives:
Filling sufficiency  research  gaps. 
Building a  community  of  trained  sufficiency ambassadors.
Working on the adoption and implementation of sufficiency  policies . 

Co-founders

Launched in 2024, the World Sufficiency Lab was created by three co-founders.

Scientific Committee

The  Scientific Committee  is composed of members of high scientific standing in sufficiency and aim at providing scientific advice and opinions on any scientific matter.

The World Sufficiency Lab is a non-profit and independent Paris-based think-to-do tank, whose mission is to serve as the primary resource and voice for sufficiency worldwide in order to reach a world in which sufficiency is first in all policy and business decisions to keep the planet healthy and liveable for all. 

To accomplish its mission, the World Sufficiency Lab pursues the following objectives:

  1. Filling sufficiency research gaps. 
  2. Building a community of trained sufficiency ambassadors.
  3. Working on the adoption and implementation of sufficiency policies.
