The World Sufficiency Lab is a non-profit and independent Paris-based think-to-do tank.It envisions a world in which sufficiency is first in all policy and business decisions to keep the planet healthy and liveable for all. 

Mission

Its mission is to serve as the primary resource and voice for sufficiency worldwide.

Strategic Objectives

To accomplish its mission, the World Sufficiency Lab pursues the following objectives:
Filling sufficiency research gaps. 
Building a community of trained sufficiency ambassadors.
Working on the adoption and implementation of sufficiency policies.
