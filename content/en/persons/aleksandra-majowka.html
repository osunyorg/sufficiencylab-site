Aleksandra MAJOWKA is a student of International Economic Policy at PSIA, Sciences Po Paris.

She is the author of two poetry books published in Polish: Material Fatigue (FDF, 2022) and Four States of Matter (FONT, 2020). Her first prose book, Belated Execution, will be published in April 2026. In her artistic work, she explores the social consequences of climate change and technological transformation.

She comes from Chełm, Poland.
