Allegra MELLI is an emerging leader in sustainable development and governance, currently pursuing a Master in International Governance and Diplomacy at Sciences Po Paris.

She holds an MSc in Complex Systems Engineering and Management from TU Delft, where she graduated cum laude.

Her academic journey spanning Kenya, the Democratic Republic of Congo, Vietnam, Australia, South Korea, the Netherlands, Italy, and France, has shaped her commitment to innovative approaches to ecological transition.

She conducted research at Fraunhofer ISI, the European Commission and the Dutch Institute for Applied Scientific Research (TNO). 

Beyond academics, Allegra is deeply engaged in youth empowerment. She aims to build a career at the intersection of international development, ecological transition, and innovation policy, working toward a more resilient global future.
