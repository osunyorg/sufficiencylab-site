Andréa Montant is the General Secretary and a Founding Member of the SSL. With a background in life sciences and sustainability engineering at EPFL (Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne) and project coordination at UNIL (University of Lausanne) for deliberative and democratic processes within academia, she plays a key role in shaping the SSL’s strategy, governance and operations. Andréa is dedicated to fostering collaboration across disciplines and sectors to turn sufficiency principles into tangible actions that support thriving communities and a sustainable future for all.
