Anna ABRAHAM is a climate communications specialist with 4 years of experience in climate and energy. She currently works as the Communications Analyst at the City Resilience Program at the World Bank in Paris. Previously, she has worked with organisations such as UNEP, REN21, Climate Action Network International and is currently completing her masters degree at Sciences Po in Environmental Policy.
