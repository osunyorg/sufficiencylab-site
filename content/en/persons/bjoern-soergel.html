Bjoern is a researcher at the Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research (PIK). He investigates the interactions between climate change, mitigation efforts, global inequality, and sustainable development. His work highlights the mutual synergies between implementing the Paris Agreement and the broader agenda of the UN Sustainable Development Goals, including targets like poverty eradication, zero hunger or biodiversity protection. He was a contributing author for WG3 Ch. 3 of the IPCC 6th Assessment Report, and has recently co-lead the modelling efforts for a model comparison of sustainable development pathway scenarios in the SHAPE project.
