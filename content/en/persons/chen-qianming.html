Chen QIANMING is a master student at SciencesPo Paris, Environmental Policy major. She has an education background in international environmental law and policy and has working experiences in international negotiation, green finance, and conservation. She has cross-stakeholder working experiences in the field of sustainability, including international organizations, environmental foundations, think tanks, local environmental NGOs, and governmental institutions. She also enjoys wildlife photography. She is now particularly interested in biodiversity conservation policy, and wishes to become a researcher on human-nature relation in the future.
