Chloé DUPRAT is a dual master's student in environmental policy at Sciences Po and environmental sciences at Sorbonne Université.

Drawing on this interdisciplinary training, she has contributed to policy development at the science-policy interface at the French Ministry of Ecology and the IPCC Working Group I Technical Support Unit.

Chloé is the founder of Zero Hour Europe, a youth-led climate NGO advocating for the global phase-out of fossil fuels.
