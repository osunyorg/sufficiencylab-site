Chris Smith is a senior research fellow at the University of Leeds. Chris is the lead developer of the simple climate model “fair”, and the co-leader of Horizon Europe funded project WorldTrans that will improve representation of human decision-making and feedbacks between human and Earth systems in a new transparent integrated assessment model. Chris was a chapter scientist and contributing author to the IPCC Sixth Assessment Report Working Group 1 and Special Report on 1.5°C, and was heavily involved in the climate assessment of emissions pathways in the Working Group 3 contribution.
