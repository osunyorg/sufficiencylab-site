Mr. Emmanuele Di Francesco is communications and events lead at the World Resources Forum, where he coordinates the WRF conferences and works on international projects related to responsible sourcing, circular economy, sufficiency and built environment. With a background in economics and philosophy, Emanuele is also the founder and editor of Circular Conversations and was previously co-founder of a vertical farming startup.
