Jose Luis HERRERA SAENZ is a Master's student in International Development at Sciences Po Paris. His research focuses on critical approaches to development, environmental justice, urban inequality, and alternatives to growth-centred economic models.

Drawing on debates around sufficiency, decolonial thought, and sustainability, he is interested in how public policy can respond to ecological limits while addressing social inequalities.

Jose gained experience in international and policy-oriented environments, which informs his interest in governance and institutional change.

Originally from Monterrey, Mexico, Jose is particularly interested in urban policy and development challenges in Latin America .
