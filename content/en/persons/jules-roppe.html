Jules ROPPE is a master's student in the Environmental Sciences and Policy Dual Degree between Sciences Po Paris and La Sorbonne.

His current academic work explores the intersection of ecological resilience, social well-being and technical feasibility. He holds a background in Engineering from l'École des Mines de Nancy, where he specialized in Energy and Material Sciences.

Jules worked on Circular Economy, LCA and Industrial Ecology, notably through circular economy reports on the first french recycling facility of radioactive matter (Technocentre, CNDP, Fessenheim)
