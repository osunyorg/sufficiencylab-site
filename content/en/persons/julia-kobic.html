Julia KOBIC is a Master's student pursuing a double degree in International Security at Sciences Po Paris and in Aerospace Engineering at ISAE-SUPAERO. She specialises in human security issues, with a research focus on the intersection between climate change and instability.

Julia believes that sufficiency offers a pathway to a safer, more stable and equitable future.

Julia has previously worked with the Centre d'Etudes Stratégiques de la Marine (CESM), ANU Centre for European Studies, the ACT Government and the Polish Embassy in Canberra.
