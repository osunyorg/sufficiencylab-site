Kai Kuhnhenn has been working for ten years at the collective Konzeptwerk Neue Ökonomie e.V. (Laboratory for new economic ideas). Previously, he worked for more than eight years at the German Federal Environment Agency on mitigation scenarios, policy evaluation, sufficiency and the coordination of national responses to IPCC reports. His main research interest lies in the combination of degrowth thoughts and concepts with climate mitigation scenarios and policies.
