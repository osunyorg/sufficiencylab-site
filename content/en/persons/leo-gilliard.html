Leo GILLIARD is Lead Science to Policy and Advocacy at WWF Switzerland, where he works at the intersection of sustainability, education, and public policy.

His work focuses on integrating ecological limits, sufficiency, and transformation competencies into education, research, and innovation frameworks at cantonal and federal levels.

Leo coordinates dialogue between NGOs, public authorities, and academic institutions, and is particularly interested in how policy tools and learning processes can support systemic change toward more sustainable and resource light ways of living.
