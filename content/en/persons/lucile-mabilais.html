Lucile MABILLAIS is a Master’s student in Environmental Policy at Sciences Po, with a specialization in the ecological transition of the agriculture and food sector. Her academic work focuses on the intersections between environmental governance, food systems, and policies for a fair transition. She interned at the French Ministry for Ecological Transition, where she worked on environmental labelling and contributed to policy work on sustainable production and consumption.
