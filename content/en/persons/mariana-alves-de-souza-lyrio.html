Mariana ALVES DE SOUZA LYRIO is a Brazilian environmental policy professional. Her most recent experience is as Policy Advisor at Observatório do Clima, Brazil's leading network of socioenvironmental NGOs.

Mariana's diverse background encompasses roles in NGOs, the private sector, and international organizations. She is also actively involved in founding and coordinating non-profit initiatives.

Mariana is currently enrolled in the Master's in Environmental Policy at Sciences Po Paris.
