Marine BERNICOT  is a qualified engineer and graduate of École Centrale de Lyon. She has over 12 years of professional experience in hydropower and water resource management, having worked in both France and several African countries. She is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Environmental Policy at Sciences Po Paris.
