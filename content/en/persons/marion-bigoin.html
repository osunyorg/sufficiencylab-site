Marion BIGOIN holds bachelor's degrees in Philosophy and Political Science from Panthéon-Sorbonne University. She is currently completing a master's degree in Environmental Policy at the School of International Affairs at Sciences Po Paris. Committed to environmental and animal ethics, she advocates for a respectful coexistence between humans and the ecosystems they are a part of.
