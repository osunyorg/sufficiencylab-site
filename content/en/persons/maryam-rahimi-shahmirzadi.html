Maryam RAHIMI SHAHMIRZADI is a Master of Public Policy student at the Munk School of Global Affairs & Public Policy, University of Toronto, with expertise in evidence-based policymaking, climate adaptation, and equitable governance.

Her professional experience includes policy research and analysis roles at the Federation of Canadian Municipalities and Equal Voice, where she developed knowledge resources and analyzed barriers to equitable representation.

Through the InnovEAT hackathon and Climate Leadership Program, she has deepened her understanding of sustainable development and systems-based policy approaches.

Maryam is committed to rigorous, community-engaged research that advances climate action and social equity.
