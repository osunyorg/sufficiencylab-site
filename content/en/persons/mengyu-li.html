Mengyu Li is a researcher at University of Sydney. Her current research includes 1) low-carbon power grids, electric vehicles and energy transition pathways; 2) quantitative assessments of climate-, pandemic- and geopolitics-related disasters; and 3) integrated assessment of future resource scenarios of climate change.
