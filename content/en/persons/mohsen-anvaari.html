Mohsen ANVAARI is an IT consultant advising public sector organizations in Norway on digitalization strategies and governance. He holds a Ph.D. in Information Technology from NTNU, with a focus on empirical studies of IT in practice. His work explores the political economy of digitalization, particularly the structural dynamics that drive centralization, dependency, and ecological impact. He is especially interested in sufficiency-oriented approaches to digital governance and in envisioning democratic, decentralized alternatives.
