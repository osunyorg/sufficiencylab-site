Nathalie Spittler is part of the Earth4All modelling team. She is a postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Global Change and Sustainability at the University of Natural Resources and Life Sciences, Vienna, where she currently co-leads the Foresight Group. She also works as a sustainability analyst for the Millennium Institute and is an active member of the Austrian chapter of The Club of Rome. Her focus lies on the application and teaching of systems thinking tools to address global challenges.
