Nilusha RATTANSI is a first-year Master's student at UBC, studying Public Policy & Global Affairs with a focus on Resources, Energy, and Sustainability. From her past experiences in both Canadian and international contexts, Nilusha developed a strong interest in the climate-health nexus, as well as human rights and the environment. Nilusha is very grateful for the opportunity to participate in the Tribunal, and is looking forward to learning from this interdisciplinary group of students and speakers about how we can collaborate effectively to address climate change.
