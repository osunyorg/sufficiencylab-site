Mr. Peter Elwin is the Director of Fixed Income & Head of Food & Land Use programme at Planet Tracker. Peter has over 20 years of financial markets experience in senior management and functional roles on the buy side and sell side. Peter was previously Head of Research at the Universities Superannuation Scheme (USS), one of the largest UK private pension schemes with over £60bn under management. Before joining USS, Peter was Deputy Head of European Research and the #1-rated Global Head of Pensions, Valuation, & Accounting research at JP Morgan.
