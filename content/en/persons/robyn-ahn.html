Robyn AHN is a first-year Master of Global Affairs student at the Munk School of Global Affairs & Public Policy at the University of Toronto. She holds a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology and Health Science and is interested in public policy design related to climate resilience, global health, and sustainable development.

Her current work focuses on applied policy research and stakeholder engagement, including work on biodiversity and sustainability initiatives through ALUS Canada, and on translating evidence into practical policy solutions.
