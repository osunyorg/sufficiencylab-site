Samuel GRZYBOWSKI is a social entrepreneur who has founded several associative and entrepreneurial organisations in the Social and Solidarity Economy (SSE) including Coexister (2009), InterFaith Tour (2012), Convivencia (2015) and, more recently, Climate Protectors (2023), an NGO that leads a community of billionaires committed to climate protection. He is currently CEO of a local cooperative of third places committed to the energy transition.

Samuel has published 3 books (Tous les chemins mènent à l'autre, l'atelier 2015; Manifeste pour une Coexistence active, l'atelier 2015; Fraternité Radicale, les Arènes 2018). He is a lecturer at Sciences Po and ESSEC.
