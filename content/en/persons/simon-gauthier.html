Simon Gauthier is a PhD candidate at the Industrial Engineering Laboratory (CentraleSupelec) and RTE (the French Electricity Transmission System Operator). He is working on scenario development and quantitative modelling of lifestyles for energy prospective.
Simon holds an Engineering degree in Energy from Mines de Nancy and a Master of Science in Physics from Imperial College, London. After more than 5 years as a consultant in energy, climate and mobility for local French authorities at BL évolution, he decided to embark in a PhD thesis which he started in 2025.
