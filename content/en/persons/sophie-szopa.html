Sophie Szopa is an atmospheric chemist at the Laboratoire des Sciences du Climat et de l'Environnement, Université Paris-Saclay. She uses numerical models to study how chemistry intervenes in the modification of the composition of the atmosphere and how this affects climate and air quality. She coordinated a chapter on short-lived chemical forces for the AR6-WGI. She participated in the synthesis report.
