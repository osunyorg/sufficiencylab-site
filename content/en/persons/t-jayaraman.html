 T. Jayaraman is a theoretical physicist by training who moved to research and teaching in climate policy research. He focuses on an trans-disciplinary approach to climate policy with a focus on equity and climate justice issues, both at the international and national level. He has worked with civil society and government in India on climate issues and has been part of the Indian delegation to the IPCC AR6 sessions and to COP and SB sessions.
