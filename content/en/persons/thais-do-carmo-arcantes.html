Thaís do CARMO ARANTES is an environmental technician from Vitória, Brasil.

Thais's academic interests and career experiences are mainly centered on the decolonization of Southern women's knowledge, particularly ecological.

She is currently following a Master in Environmental Policy at Sciences Po, Paris.
