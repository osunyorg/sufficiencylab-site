Valentin Brandon holds a master's degree in Geopolitics and Foresight, Management, and Public Administration. He also has a background in Sport and Education Sciences. His work is focused on promoting sufficiency as a paradigmatic concept, with a particular interest in its democratic dimension: empowering citizens and fostering meaningful participation in public affairs by reorienting the economy from accumulation toward meeting human needs.
