Victoire PAYER is a second-year master's student in environmental policy at the Paris School of International Affairs. Her master's program allowed her to specialize in European studies and biodiversity. She completed her bachelor’s degree at Sciences Po in the European-North America program. During her bachelor’s, she did an exchange year at the University of Montreal, where she was able to study environmental policies in depth. She has been involved in several organizations aimed at raising public awareness, particularly among children, about biodiversity conservation. Currently, she is completing an apprenticeship at the Urban Ecology Agency of the City of Paris. Next year, she hopes to work in the public sector or at an NGO on projects aimed at protecting biodiversity.
