Xinzhi Li is a graduate candidate in International Energy Transition at Sciences Po Paris. She has an academic background in Political Science and Data Analytics and has worked across China and France in international organizations, research institutes, and a chamber of commerce.

Xinzhi is currently a Climate Expert Intern at Greenly, focusing on climate data and sustainability.

Beyond her professional interests, she is passionate about theatre and filmmaking, and enjoys hiking and running.
